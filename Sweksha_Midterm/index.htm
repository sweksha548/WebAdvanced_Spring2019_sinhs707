    VR EXPERIENCE & GAME

    The Northern Lights are predicted to become colourless in a few decades due to global warming.Through the VR experience, I want people to realize how beautiful and mesmerizing the Northern Lights are!


    Last year was my first fall! I was so excited and waiting to see all the fall colours. But the nature had other plans! The fall this year was very short and New York didn’t witness a lot of fall colours. The leaves turned yellow and then fell off.

    The VR Experience 'Where did Orange go?' is an adaption on the prediction of loss of colours from the nature in the future!

    The VR experience is an experience that makes the user feel what the world would look like devoid of the colours of nature.Would we be okay to live in world devoid of the scenic beauty of nature?
